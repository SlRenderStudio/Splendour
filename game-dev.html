JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Creativity and Originality: How unique and innovative the game concept is.
  • Theme Integration: How well the game incorporates the announced theme.
  • Gameplay and Fun: How enjoyable and engaging the game is.
  • Technical Achievement: Complexity and technical execution of the game.
  • Art and Audio: Quality and coherence of visuals and sound.
  • Presentation: Overall polish and presentation of the game.
