Game Development

THEME

Robotics: Participants must explore the theme of robotics in their submissions.

ELIGIBILITY AND PARTICIPATION

  • The competition will be held as two catogories
    -6-9: Children aged 6 to 9 years old.
    -10-13: Children aged 10 to 13 years old
  • Participants must participate individualy.

GUIDELINES

  • Participants must create a game that prominently features robotics as a central theme. The game can explore any aspect of robotics such as automation, AI, futuristic robots, robot competitions, or ethical considerations of robotics in society.
  • The game should be developed for specify platform(s) such as PC, mobile, or web.
  • It must be original work, and all assets (graphics, audio, code) should either be original or properly attributed if third-party resources are used.
  • Games should be playable and submitted as executable files or accessible via a provided URL

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Creativity and Innovation: How uniquely the game integrates the theme of robotics.
  • Gameplay Mechanics: How engaging and well-implemented are the game mechanics.
  • Visuals and Audio: Quality of graphics and sound design relative to the theme.
  • Technical Achievement: Complexity and quality of programming, AI (if applicable), and overall execution.
  • Overall Experience: How enjoyable and compelling the game is as a whole.
